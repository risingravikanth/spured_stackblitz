Features

Ask, Share and support any information/learning content with Post, Comment, Like, Favorite.

Boards are organized spaces in colleges specific to department, batch, class, section or whole college of students, Share content in any board based on whom the content is intended to.

Groups helps in keeping the content visible only to specific set of audience. Administration groups, faculty groups, course groups, event groups are some examples.

Beyond college content organized in various sections and models helps focused preparation for competitive exams.

Get latest updates on events, job notifications, news, current affairs, results, competitive exam material.

Email and app notifications which you can control, to get updates as the content gets uploaded to the platform.

Gist

Spured is a learning management system for college & students to share anything related to education & college. Boards are the hierarchy of spaces with students belongs to various batches, departments, classes and sections. A student of a class belong to his/her class board, department board, batch board and college board.

Groups in the college (institute groups) are useful to create groups for various topics of discussions such as subject groups, library group, college fest group and so on.

Spured is also a knowledge networking platform to connect with scholars beyond the college. Content or questions related various sections or models of competitive exams, results, jobb opportunities, news, current affairs, any kind of events, higher education. Public groups are meant for discussion on any topic, anyone can join any public group and view content in that group. Various sections of the competitive exams are organized into categories and models to find the content what exactly needed.
